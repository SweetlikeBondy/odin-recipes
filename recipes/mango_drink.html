Summertime Mango Drink

Discription

a refreshing burst of tropical goodness in every sip. Made from sun-ripened, juicy mangoes, this vibrant drink delivers the perfect balance of natural sweetness and tangy citrus undertones.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 â…” cups milk

Steps

  1. Combine mangoes, ice cubes, vanilla extract, and sugar in a blender.
  2. Pour in milk.
  3. Blend until smooth and serve.
